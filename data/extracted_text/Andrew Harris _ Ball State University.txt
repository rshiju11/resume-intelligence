Andrew Harris | Ball State University
Andy Harris has taught computer science in central Indiana for 30 years. He loves to teach freshman computing and game
development. Some of his research and teaching interests include how to use game development to reinforce learning of
computer programming and mathematics, and serious gaming. He also is interested in the use of game development
techniques to help solve real-world problems in education and industry.
