i'm on leave, and have moved to nyu, my updated webpage is here.
i'm a faculty member at tel aviv university's school of computer science. i'm interested in the theory of
computation at large, and the theory of cryptography in particular.
before joining tau i was a postdoctoral researcher at mit csail hosted by vinod vaikuntanathan. i completed
my ph.d. at tel aviv university where i was advised by ran canetti.
