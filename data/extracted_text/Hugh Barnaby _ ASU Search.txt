long bio
hugh barnaby joined the arizona state university faculty in .
he has served as an active researcher in the microelectronics field
for more than 13 years in both industry and academics, presenting
and publishing more than 60 papers during this time. prior to asu,
he was an assistant professor at the university of arizona. he has
also worked as a staff scientist for the microelectronics division at
mission reseach corporation in albuquerque, n.m. where he
performed radiation effects and reliability analysis on vlsi digital
and analog/mixed signal circuits.
professor barnaby's research interests include semiconductors for
hostile environments, device physics and modeling,
microelectronic device and sensor design and manufacturing,
and analog/rf/mixed signal circuit design.
